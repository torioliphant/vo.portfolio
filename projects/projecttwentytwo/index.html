I will live forever on the internet

Installation / Concept Album

Adobe Premiere Pro, Final Cut, Processing, p5.js, InDesign, Photoshop, Illustrator, Canva

VISIT SITE↗

I WILL LIVE FOREVER, ON THE INTERNET is a visual concept album exploring the internet’s impact on identity, permanence, and self-performance highlighting themes of digital intimacy and online obsession, the work merges original music with film and generative code. Rooted in the nostalgia of early internet aesthetics, the installation contrasts the physical decay of outdated hardware with the infinite nature of the digital realm. What does it mean to haunt the internet? Forever archived. Forever performing.

Branding, Creative Direction, Video Production, JavaScript, Audio-Visual Code, Generative, Editorial Design, Original Music Composition
