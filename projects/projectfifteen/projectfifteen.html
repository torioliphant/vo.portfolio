RW

EDITORIAL
Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe After Effects

RW is an art book dedicated to Rufus Wainwright’s song Go or Go Ahead, exploring artist branding and type design. It showcases a series of typography experiments, beginning with simple black-and-white layouts and gradually incorporating color, images, and animation—all using a single font, Bodoni.
