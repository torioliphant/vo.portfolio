Brilliant Traces

Live Theater Projection

QLab, Premiere Pro, Adobe After Effects, Photoshop, Illustrator, Canva, Caput

VISIT SITE↗

Live at the Hudson Theatre, Brilliant Traces, a story about grief and memory, captivated audiences through careful attention to narrative and design. Brought on as a projection designer, I oversaw the creative direction of full-stage video content that supported the intimate, two-character drama. The process involved compiling and editing 100+ video assets from found footage, actor-sourced material, and abstract collage, creating multimedia assets. I programmed time-based cues in QLab and provided on-site technical direction throughout the run, adjusting color grading in real-time and advising on set design / spatial layout. This project demanded fluency across multi-software workflows while maintaining the delicate balance between supporting the actors and enhancing the story's emotional landscape.

Projection Design, Art Direction, Video Content Creation, Set Design, Cue Programming, Lighting Design, Motion Graphics, Color Grading
