SHIRLEY SMOKES

Product Design
Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign, Adobe Photoshop, Figma

Shirley Smokes Candy Cigarettes" is a playful nod to 1920s flapper culture, blending vintage comic book aesthetics with a hand-drawn illustration of Shirley, a confident, free-spirited character. The branding features custom lettering and period-inspired embellishments, reinforcing the era’s bold style. Designing the packaging and visual identity, I wanted to highlight 1920's whimsy – with the goal to create a lighthearted, healthy alternative to smoking.
