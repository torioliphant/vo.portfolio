RODNEY

3D CHARACTER
Blender, Creality Scan, PolyCam, Adobe Substance 3D Painter

Rodney started as an aluminum wire skeleton, built up with foil and clay before being scanned into 3D. The process involved re-texturing, re-modeling, and lastly adding animation.
